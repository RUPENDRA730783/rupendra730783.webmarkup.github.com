Top Destinations in Nepal

  • Kathmandu – The Cultural Capital

    Kathmandu is known for its temples, squares, and ancient art. Visit Swayambhunath, Boudhanath, and Patan Durbar Square to experience centuries-old traditions and architecture.

  • Pokhara – The City of Lakes

    Pokhara offers breathtaking views of the Annapurna range and Phewa Lake. Enjoy paragliding, boating, or hiking to the World Peace Pagoda — perfect for relaxation and adventure alike.

  • Chitwan – Gateway to Jungle Adventures

    Explore Chitwan National Park and spot rhinos, elephants, and Bengal tigers. It’s Nepal’s premier destination for wildlife and jungle experiences.
