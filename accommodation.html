Accommodation Options in Nepal

  • Hotels – Kathmandu & Pokhara

    In major cities like Kathmandu and Pokhara, you’ll find international-standard hotels offering comfort, modern amenities, and excellent service. These are perfect for travelers who want convenience and easy access to restaurants, shops, and cultural sites.

  • Homestays – Cultural Immersion

    Homestays provide a unique opportunity to experience Nepalese hospitality. Staying with a local family allows travelers to learn about traditional customs, enjoy home-cooked meals, and connect with the culture on a personal level.

  • Eco Lodges – Nature Retreats

    For nature lovers, eco-lodges offer sustainable stays in stunning natural settings. Many are located in national parks or remote mountain areas, bringing one closer to Nepal's rich biodiversity and scenic beauty.

  • Guest Houses – Cozy Local Comfort

    For travelers seeking a warm and affordable stay, guesthouses offer a charming alternative to hotels. Often family-run and located in both cities and rural areas, these accommodations provide a relaxed atmosphere, personalized service, and a chance to connect with local hosts.
